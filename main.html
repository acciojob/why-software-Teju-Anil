Why I want to become a developer?

Passion for Technology: If you're fascinated by technology, enjoy problem-solving, and have a curiosity about how things work behind the scenes in software and applications, a career as a developer can be incredibly fulfilling. Problem-Solving: Developers face a wide range of challenges, from debugging code to optimizing performance. If you enjoy tackling complex problems and finding elegant solutions, a career as a developer can be intellectually stimulating.
